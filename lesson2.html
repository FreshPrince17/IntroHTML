What is Computing?

Computing, in the context of computer science, refers to the process of using computer systems to solve problems. This involves the design and use of algorithms (step-by-step procedures), data structures (ways to organize and store data), and computer systems (hardware and software) to efficiently process, analyze, or transmit information. It's a broad field that includes areas like programming, data analysis, artificial intelligence, network design, and more.
